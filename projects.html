SmartBizSeeker

  Smart BizSeeker is a recommender system designed to help businesses and organisations make faster and better decisions by providing targeted recommendations for potential partners/suppliers/customers.

  In essence, Smart BizSeeker provides “personalised recommendations” at an organisational level.

  It is able to accurately predict user’s (i.e. organisation’s) preferences and automatically provide targeted partner recommendations.

  Smart BizSeeker can be applied to a range of complex recommendation needs, including business-to business (B2B), government-to-business (G2B) and business-to-researcher (B2R).
